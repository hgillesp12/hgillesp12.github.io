Hannah Gillespie

Masters student in Engineering for Sustainable Development at the University of Cambridge. Former Autonomous Systems Engineer at Boeing in Seattle. Interested in developing robotic systems for applications related to sustainability within the context of human-centered design thinking. Completed the MSc in Computing at Imperial College London with first-class distinction. Notre Dame (USA) alum from 2020, originally from the Chicagoland area.

In my free time, I enjoy rowing on the River Cam, playing Chopin on the piano, and searching for a coffee shop with a La Marzocco espresso machine.
